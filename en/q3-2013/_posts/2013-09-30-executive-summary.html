Executive Summary: 3 Key Trends in LRA Activity

1. Sharp decrease in LRA killings and abductions

LRA forces killed two civilians in Q3 2013 - following 29 killings in Q2 and 28 killings in Q1 - the lowest number of civilian deaths per quarter in the LRA Crisis Tracker dataset. No civilian deaths were reported in DRC. Abductions and returnees both fell by roughly 50% quarter over quarter, from 136 to 67 abductions and 110 to 54 returnees from Q2 to Q3 2013.

2. LRA attacks rise in DRC, fall in CAR

Q3 held steady at 36 LRA attacks, down only 2 from the previous quarter. Attacks rose by 10 in DRC (21 to 31), and fell by more than 17 attacks in CAR (17 to 5). Despite this fall, CAR contained the most violent LRA attack, with 2 killed and 34 abducted. (Discussion of armed groups)
